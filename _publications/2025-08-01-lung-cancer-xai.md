This work originated as an undergraduate final year project (2022) at **Kathford International College of Engineering and Management, Lalitpur, Nepal** (affiliated with Tribhuvan University), and was later written up and posted as an arXiv preprint in 2025.

We benchmark CNN architectures — DenseNet121, ResNet152, VGG19, and a custom CNN — for lung cancer detection from CT scan images, and apply SHAP-based post-hoc explainability to identify discriminative image regions. A poster presentation of this work received the **Best Poster Award** at the 16th NeSA International Conference, 2025.
