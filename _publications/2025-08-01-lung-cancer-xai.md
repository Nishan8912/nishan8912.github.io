We develop and benchmark multiple CNN architectures — DenseNet121, ResNet152, VGG19, and a custom CNN — for lung cancer detection from CT scan images. We apply SHAP-based post-hoc explainability to identify discriminative CT scan regions, improving model trustworthiness for clinical users. This work received the **Best Poster Award** at the 16th NeSA International Conference, 2025.
