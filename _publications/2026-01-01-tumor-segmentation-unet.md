We develop an Attention U-Net framework for tumor segmentation that is transferable across MRI (BraTS dataset) and CT (LIDC-IDRI dataset) modalities. We design custom hybrid loss functions (Dice + Focal) and advanced augmentation strategies to address class imbalance in medical imaging datasets, achieving competitive Dice scores across both modalities.
